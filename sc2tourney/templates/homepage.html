GL HF!

The PyCon 2012 SC2 tournament is coming. Everyone/anyone can play, regardless of experience level, just for fun (and minor prizes). We'll try to pair the first round by hand based on skill level.

Rules

Entry
Entry is free but you must register by 2PM on Friday
Eligible Maps
  • Cloud Kingdom LE (2)
  • Entombed Valley (4)
  • Antiga Shipyard (4)
  • Metalopolis (4) (no close spawns)
  • Korhal Compound LE (2)
  • Shakuras Plateau (4) (no close spawns)
  • Tal'darim Altar LE (4)
  • The Shattered Temple (4) (no close spawns)
Eligibility
We're trying to schedule an event for the finals, so you must be present at PyCon to enter. Depending on the number of entrants, and our ability to acquire a location, some rounds may happen asychronously.
Gameplay
Best of 3 — lower ranked (or coin toss if equal) picks first map, loser picks after.
Prizes
  • $25 amazon gift card for the winner [Kelly Creative Tech]
  • $15 best match (determined by judges, who might be playing, judges can't vote on their own games) [Kelly Creative Tech]
  • One T-Shirt from Ownaj for 1st and 2nd place [Toast Driven]
Other
  • You MUST save and upload replays.
  • You agree that we can make videos and cast the games
  • If you'd like to cast some game(s) or donate a prize, or help, let us know @issackelly or @daniellindsley.
  • Join the 'pycon' chatroom on Battle.net or on Disqus's PyCon Conversation Tool
